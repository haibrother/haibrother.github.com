INET_ATON()和INET_NTOA() ip转换

INET_ATON将IP转换INT,INET_NTOA 将转换成原始IP

mysql> select INET_ATON('192.168.2.1') from member \G
*************************** 1. row ***************************
INET_ATON('192.168.2.1'): 3232236033

mysql> select INET_NTOA('3232236033') from member \G
*************************** 1. row ***************************
INET_NTOA('3232236033'): 192.168.2.1
